02 / 选址 5 规则5 siting rules 悬停看原因Hover for why
1
离地 1.5-3mHeight 1.5-3 m
为什么: 太低吃汽车尾气 + 灰尘, 太高代表性差. 与人呼吸高度一致 (PurpleAir 官方建议). Why: too low = car exhaust & dust; too high = unrepresentative. Matches breathing height (PurpleAir official guide).
2
避热源Avoid heat sources
为什么: 烧烤架/暖气出风口/排油烟 = 极端 PM 爆发, 不代表你想测的"空气", 数据被毁. Why: grills, heat vents & range exhaust dump extreme PM that's not the ambient air you want to measure.
3
远空调外机Away from AC unit
为什么: 外机吹出强气流 + 湿热, 干扰 PM 传感器气泵采样, 读数偏离真实环境. Why: AC outdoor units blast hot humid airflow that disturbs the PM sensor's intake fan & biases readings.
4
通风良好Good airflow
为什么: 死角空气滞留, 测出来是"小气泡"局部空气, 不是社区大气. 阳台/院子开口处最好. Why: dead corners trap a local bubble of air, not the neighborhood air. Place at balcony/yard openings.
5
离地 > 1mAbove 1 m off ground
为什么: 地面扬尘/宠物毛/草坪粉尘会让读数虚高, 1m 以上才接近"空气质量". (与 1 规则配合 → 优 1.5m+) Why: ground dust, pet hair & lawn particles inflate PM readings; >1m gets closer to true ambient air.
