从想动手到角色真动起来 From imagining a move to a character actually moving

从想动手到角色真动起来，中间隔着这五步。先看清这条总链路，再沿 S1-S6 一步步把它做出来。 Between wanting to act and the character moving sit these five steps. See the whole chain first, then build it across S1-S6.

总链路 · 意图 / 信号 / 采集 / 解码 / 指令Pipeline · intent / signal / capture / decode / command
01
脑想象Motor imagery
想象左/右手动imagine left/right move
你不用真的动，只在脑子里想象「左手动」或「右手动」。运动皮层会因此产生不同的活动模式。You don't move — you just imagine moving your left or right hand. The motor cortex reacts with distinct patterns.
S1在 S1 认识脑波时学Learned in S1: meeting brain waves
02
脑波信号Brain signal
微伏级电压波microvolt waves
大量神经元一起放电，在头皮上形成极其微弱的电压波动（μV 级），这就是 EEG 脑电信号。Many neurons firing together create tiny voltage ripples on the scalp (microvolts) — the EEG signal.
S1在 S1 看懂波形与频段Learned in S1: waveforms and bands
03
电极采集Electrode capture
头戴电极 + 清洁headset + cleaning
头戴电极把信号读进电脑，但里面混着眨眼、肌肉、工频等噪声，必须先过滤和清洁。Electrodes read the signal into the computer, but it's full of blink, muscle and power-line noise that must be filtered first.
S2在 S2 学清洁与滤波Learned in S2: cleaning and filtering
04
CSP + LDA 解码CSP + LDA decode
提特征 + 分类器features + classifier
CSP 把左右手想象的差异放大成特征，LDA 分类器据此判断你想的是「左」还是「右」。CSP amplifies the left-vs-right difference into features; the LDA classifier decides whether you imagined "left" or "right".
S3在 S3 找特征、训练分类器Learned in S3: features + training a classifier
05
游戏指令Game command
角色真动起来character moves
分类结果转成「左移 / 右移」指令发给游戏，角色就跟着你的念头动起来——闭环完成。The result becomes a "move left / right" command to the game, and the character moves with your thought — loop closed.
S5在 S5 接入 MinecraftLearned in S5: connecting to Minecraft
