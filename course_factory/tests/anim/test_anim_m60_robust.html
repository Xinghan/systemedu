现场校准与扩展演练 — 走出实验室 Field Calibration & Stress Test — Out of the Lab

之前所有数据都在「你家书桌 + 你状态好 + 你自己脑袋」里录的。今天我们把系统拖进真实世界——换时间、换地点、换被试，看它到底鲁不鲁棒。 Every dataset so far came from "your desk + your good day + your own head". Today we drag it into the real world — new times, places and subjects — to see how robust it really is.

核心概念Core idea
鲁棒性 = 抗变化能力Robustness = resistance to change
实验室里 95% 的准确率，只证明它在「最理想的那一套条件」下成立。真鲁棒，是把下面三个前提一个个抽掉后还能跑。95% in the lab only proves it works under one perfect setup. Real robustness means it survives after you pull each of these three assumptions away.

ANOVA 方差分析 · 差异是真的还是偶然ANOVA · real gap or noise
同一组数总会有波动。方差分析比较「组间差异」和「组内波动」，算出 F 值和 p 值：p < 0.05 才说明这个因素真的影响了准确率。Numbers always wobble. ANOVA compares between-group gaps vs within-group wobble, giving F and p. Only p < 0.05 means the factor truly moves accuracy.
时段Time F=2.1 p=.14 不显著not sig.
地点Place F=11.4 p=.002 显著significant
被试Subject F=18.7 p<.001 显著significant
读法：时段几乎没影响（早晚都能用）；地点和换人才是真正的拦路虎——户外噪声和别人的脑波，是你系统真正的边界。Time barely matters (works morning or night). Place and a new person are the real walls — outdoor noise and another brain mark your system's true boundary.
