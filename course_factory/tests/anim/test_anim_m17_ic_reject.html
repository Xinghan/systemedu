认出哪一路是眨眼，把它扔掉

ICA 拆出好几路独立成分。眨眼那一路藏不住三个签名：前额对称的头皮分布、几百毫秒的大慢峰、低频能量特别高。三者吻合，就锁定它、剔除它。 ICA yields several components. The blink one carries three tell-tale signatures — frontal symmetric scalp map, a slow large peak, low-freq heavy spectrum. When all three agree, mark it and drop it.
