MY-AIRNODE-01 · WEEK 26
~ 450 words · 1 page

我家小区今天空气多少 — 26 周以来我学到的 What's the Air Like at My Place — 26 Weeks of Learning

1
第 1 段 · 我做了一台传感器 (1 句话说清, 别讲焊接细节) Para 1 · I built a sensor (one sentence, skip soldering)
EG 我用了一个塑料盒 · 装上小风扇和探头 · 挂在阳台 · 它现在每分钟数一次空气里有多脏。 I put a fan and a probe in a plastic box · hung it on the balcony · now every minute it counts how dirty the air is.
2
第 2 段 · 第一周记忆最深的发现 (有具体场景) Para 2 · The most striking thing from week 1 (be specific)
EG 我以为早上空气最好 · 结果第一周就看到 — 上下班高峰那两小时 · PM2.5 翻了一倍。 I thought morning air was cleanest · but week 1 showed me — during rush hour PM2.5 doubled.
3
第 3 段 · 26 周里最意外的事 (反直觉 = 好故事) Para 3 · The biggest surprise in 26 weeks (counter-intuitive wins)
EG 下雨前 · 我的节点经常先变红 · 我查了下 — 是湿度让小颗粒变胖 · 探头才数到的不是真的更脏。 Before rain · the node often goes red first · I looked it up — humidity makes particles "puff up" so the probe counts them as more.
4
第 4 段 · 我现在对空气的感觉 (从陌生到熟悉) Para 4 · How air feels to me now (from stranger to friend)
EG 以前看天就一种灰 · 现在我会看一眼节点 · 心里说一句 "今天可以骑车" 或 "今天关窗"。 The sky used to be just "grey" to me · now I glance at the node and tell myself "today bike OK" or "today windows shut".
5
第 5 段 · 一句给同学的话 (留个钩子 · 让 ta 想做) Para 5 · One line to a classmate (leave a hook · make them want to)
EG 如果你也想知道你家空气什么样 · 你可以做一个 · 一个塑料盒 + 一个周末 · 就够了。 If you want to know your own air · you can build one · a plastic box + one weekend · enough.
BY ME · WEEK 26 WEEK 26 —— 我自己 写 —— signed, myself
写作贴士 · 3 条 3 Writing Tips
用故事 · 不用结论 Tell a story · not a conclusion
"那天早上 8 点 · 我看到红色" "that 8am · I saw red" · "高峰时段污染严重" "peak hours severely polluted"
给数字配个比喻 Pair a number with a metaphor
"PM2.5 = 80 · 像在车后面跟着走" "PM2.5 = 80 · like walking behind a car" · "PM2.5 浓度 80 μg/m³" "PM2.5 concentration 80 μg/m³"
别用术语 · 5 年级听得懂 No jargon · 5th-grader has to get it
"探头每分钟数一次脏东西" "probe counts the dirt every minute" · "激光散射粒子计数采样" "laser scattering particle count sampling"
