先用 CSP 把左右手想象的差异放大成几个特征，再用 LDA 画一条直线分两类。经典、轻巧，几十条数据就能训出能用的分类器。 CSP amplifies the left-vs-right difference into a few features; LDA draws a line to split two classes. Classic and light — a few dozen trials already train a usable classifier.

RIEMANN 黎曼几何分类Riemanniancovariance manifold
直接拿协方差矩阵在弯曲的几何空间里量距离分类。准确率常常最高、最稳，但数学更绕，要看懂背后的流形不容易。 Classifies covariance matrices by measuring distance on a curved geometric space. Often the most accurate and stable, but the math bends harder — the manifold is tough to picture.
